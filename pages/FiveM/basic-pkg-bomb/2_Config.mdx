## Configurations
Change some minor things to ensure that the script fit your need.

## Item
The name of the item that the player needs to have to plant a bomb.

```lua copy
Config.Item = 'package_bomb'
```

- Item: `string`

## Prop
The prop that will be spawned, once the player has planted the bomb.

```lua copy
Config.Prop = "prop_cs_box_clothes"
```
