# FiveM Advanced Trucker Job

The FiveM Trucker Job Script allows for easy and efficient creation of routes for truck drivers, with a strong focus on enhancing the player experience. The script offers a wide range of features and options to customize the trucker job and maximize enjoyment. It provides a seamless and immersive gameplay experience, ensuring hours of engaging gameplay for all trucking enthusiasts.

## Installation

<Steps>
    ### Purchase
    To purchase the Script, go to the [Shop](https://shop.trusted-studios.eu/package/5697526) and purchase it. 

    ### Download
    After the purchase tebex will send you an email whereupon you can download the script from [Keymaster](https://keymaster.fivem.net/assets)

    ### Installation
    Drag and Drop the resource into your server-files and go to the configuration file located at `/conig.lua`.

    ### Configuration
    Please make sure to configure the script to your need to ensure the best experience. More information can be found [here](./2_Config).
</Steps>
